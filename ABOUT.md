## Brand

**Slow Down, Aesthetic.**

Working interpretation (Pranav — correct this if off):

- **Slow Down** — deliberate over frantic. Considered decisions, calm delivery,
  depth over volume. The opposite of the "ship fast, move fast" clichés most
  developer portfolios lead with. Craft takes the time it takes.
- **Aesthetic** — the visual and experiential bar matters. Not decoration:
  restraint, whitespace, typography, things feeling *finished*. "Pixel-tight"
  isn't a feature, it's a value.

What this should mean for the site: the copy and pacing of the site itself are
brand evidence. If the hero shouts hustle-culture metrics at the viewer, it
contradicts the brand even if it looks clean.
